Pizza

Ingredients

  • Cheeses
  • Meats
  • Veggies
  • Tomato sauce
  • Any other thing you wanna add pizzas are really personal(no pineapples)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Shape the dough in a circular form
  4. Add the sauce,
  5. Add the cheeses
  6. Add the meats
  7. Add the Veggies
  8. Add any other thing you wanna add (No Pineapples)
  9. Bake the Pizza for 16mins at 500F
  10. Enjoy
