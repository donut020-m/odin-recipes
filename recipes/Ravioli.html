Ravioli

Ingredients

  • cheeses
  • tomato sauce
  • Veggies
  • eggs
  • olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 5 cups all purpose fluor

Instructions

  1. Make the dough
  2. Prepare the filling (Cheeses, tomato sauce and veggies if u want)
  3. Prepare the pasta sheet
  4. Pass the pasta sheet through the machine
  5. Cut the raviolis
  6. Seal the raviolis
  7. Cook for 4 minutes 5 if frozen
  8. Enjoy
