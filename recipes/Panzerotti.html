Panzerotti

recipe created for people that wanna cook something new and discover the pleasure of italian food

Ingredients

  • Cheeses
  • Meats
  • Veggies
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Shape the dough in a pizza circular form
  4. Add the sauce,
  5. Add the cheeses
  6. Add the meats
  7. Add the Veggies
  8. Add any other thing you wanna add (No Pineapples)
  9. Fold the dough by the middle
  10. Bake the Panzerotti for 15mins at 500F
  11. Enjoy
